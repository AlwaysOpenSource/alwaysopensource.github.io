About Us

The AlwaysOpenSource Initiative

A.O.S. takes community suggestions and turns them into fully realized, open source projects using a team of volunteer experts. We believe everybody benefits when projects can be freely used, modified, and improved.

Made up of a rotating team, A.O.S. recruits experts from all fields to collaborate on projects.

Anyone can submit an idea to the A.O.S. team. Credit is always equally shared between the A.O.S. team and you!

All our projects will be freely available under the GNU AGPLv3 license. Anyone can use them provided their project is also open source.
