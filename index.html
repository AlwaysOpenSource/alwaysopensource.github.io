About Us

Support Open Source Projects

AlwaysOpenSource is a project that takes community suggestions and turns them into fully realized open source projects. Everybody benefits when projects can be freely used, modified, and improved by anyone in the world!

Made up of a rotating team, AlwaysOpenSource recruits people from all fields to collaborate on projects.

Anyone can submit an idea to the AlwaysOpenSource team. Credit is always equally shared between the AlwaysOpenSource team and you!

All our projects will be freely available under the GNU AGPLv3 license. Anyone to use them provided their project is also open source.
